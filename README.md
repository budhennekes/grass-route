# Grass Route

A cozy pixel-art lawn-care hustle sim. Mow lawns, dodge suburban chaos, get paid, and try not to spend your savings on mower skins.

**Play it:** https://budhennekes.github.io/grass-route/

Best on a phone in portrait, but works on desktop too.

## How to play
- **Move:** drag anywhere on the grass (or WASD / arrow keys)
- **Pick up:** the gold button, or `E` / `Space` / `Enter`
- **Finish early:** `DONE` button or `F` — banking fast with enough cut earns a speed bonus

Each lawn is a profit puzzle, not a completion puzzle. Cut what pays, skip what hurts. Progress saves in your browser.
